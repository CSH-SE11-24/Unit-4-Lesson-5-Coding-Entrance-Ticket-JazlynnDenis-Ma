About Raccoons

Raccoons are medium-sized mammals native to North America. They are known for their distinctive black mask and ringed tail. Raccoons are highly adaptable and can be found in urban, suburban, and wilderness areas.

These intelligent creatures are known for their curiosity and problem-solving skills. Raccoons are omnivores and have a varied diet that includes fruits, nuts, insects, small mammals, and even human food scraps.
